Oven-Roasted Pork Tenderloin

This meal features oven-roasted pork tenderloin as the star, cooked to perfection with delightful seasoning. Alongside, you'll enjoy zucchini noodles for a healthy twist, sautéed asparagus for a burst of freshness, and a comforting side of Rice-A-Roni pasta. It's a well-rounded and satisfying dinner, combining savory, tender, and delicious elements for a delightful dining experience.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

Oven-Roasted Pork Tenderloin

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. In a small bowl, combine the dried thyme, paprika, salt, and black pepper.

3. Rub the pork tenderloin with olive oil and then sprinkle the spice mixture evenly over the pork.

4. Place the seasoned pork tenderloin in a roasting pan and roast in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C). Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

Zucchini Noodles

1. Peel zucchinis using spiralizer or with potato peeler.

2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the zucchini noodles, season with salt and black pepper, and sauté for about 3-4 minutes until they are tender but still have a slight crunch.

Sautéed Asparagus

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the trimmed asparagus and season with salt and black pepper.

2. Sauté the asparagus for about 5-7 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly browned.

Rice-A-Roni Pasta

1. Follow the instructions on the Rice-A-Roni package.

Serve

Slice the oven-roasted pork tenderloin and serve it alongside the zucchini noodles, sautéed asparagus, and prepared Rice-A-Roni pasta.

ingredients

Oven-Roasted Pork Tenderloin

1 pork tenderloin (about 12-16 ounces)

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon paprika

Salt and black pepper to taste

Zucchini Noodles

2 medium-sized zucchinis, spiralized into noodles

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and black pepper to taste

Sautéed Asparagus

1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed

2 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and black pepper to taste

Rice-A-Roni

1 box of Rice-A-Roni (flavor of your choice)
