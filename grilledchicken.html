Grilled Honey Garlic Chicken

This delightful meal features grilled honey garlic chicken as the main course, boasting a perfect blend of sweet and savory flavors. On the side, there are crispy and satisfying zucchini chips, which are fried to a golden perfection, providing a delightful crunch. Completing the ensemble is a creamy alfredo pasta, rich with Parmesan and buttery notes, making for a comforting and indulgent accompaniment. Together, these components create a balanced and satisfying dinner that caters to a variety of taste preferences.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

Grilled Honey Garlic Chicken

1. In a bowl, whisk together honey, minced garlic, soy sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

2. Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes.

3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through. Brush with extra marinade during grilling.

Fried Zucchini Chips

1. In one bowl, place the flour. In another bowl, beat the egg. In a third bowl, combine breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.

2. Dip the zucchini slices in flour, then egg, and finally coat with the breadcrumb mixture.

3. In a deep skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Fry the zucchini slices until they are golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Knorr Pasta Alfredo

Follow the instructions on the Knorr Pasta Alfredo package.

Serve

Plate the grilled honey garlic chicken alongside a serving of fried zucchini chips and a portion of alfredo pasta. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired.

ingredients

Grilled Honey Garlic Chicken

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

2 tablespoons honey

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Fried Zucchini Chips

1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced into rounds

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 cup breadcrumbs

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1 egg

Vegetable oil for frying

Salt and pepper to taste

Knorr Pasta Alfredo

1 package of Knorr Pasta Alfredo mix

1/2 cup Milk

1 Tbsp Butter

1 1/2 cup Water
