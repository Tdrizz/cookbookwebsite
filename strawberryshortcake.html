Strawberry Shortcake

Strawberry shortcake is a timeless dessert with layers of tender shortcake, fresh strawberries, and airy whipped cream. The ripe strawberries are sweetened and macerated for extra flavor. This dessert is a perfect balance of textures and flavors, with juicy strawberries, buttery shortcake, and creamy whipped cream in every bite, making it a delightful treat enjoyed year-round.

prep time

20 min

assembly time

10 min

serving

2 servings

instructions

strawberries

1. In a bowl, combine the sliced strawberries and granulated sugar. Let them macerate for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the strawberries to release their juices and become sweet and syrupy.

whipped cream

1. In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

assembly

1. Grab 2 shortcakes, set one on a plate and the other to the side for now.

2. Spoon a generous portion of the macerated strawberries onto each shortcake.

3. Top with a generous dollop of whipped cream.

4. Place the second shortcake on top and add another dollop of whipped cream and a strawberry slice for garnish.

ingredients

Store-bought shortcakes (such as Franz)

1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced

1/4 cup granulated sugar

1 cup heavy whipping cream

2 tablespoons powdered sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla extract
