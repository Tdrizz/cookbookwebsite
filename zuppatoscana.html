Zuppa Toscana

Zuppa Toscana is a hearty Italian soup featuring spicy Italian sausage, tender potatoes, and fresh kale in a creamy broth. It's a comforting bowl of flavors and textures, with a savory kick from the sausage and a touch of green freshness from the kale. Cream adds a luscious finish, making it a beloved and satisfying choice for a comforting meal.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

Sausage and Bacon

1. In a large pot, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it into small pieces as it browns. Once cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside.

2. In the same pot, add the chopped bacon and cook until it's crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside with the cooked sausage.

Soup

1. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and cook until it becomes translucent, about 5 minutes.

2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

3. Return the cooked sausage and bacon to the pot.

4. Add the diced potatoes, chicken broth, and water. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to simmer. Cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.

5. Stir in the chopped kale and cook for an additional 5 minutes, or until the kale is wilted and tender.

6. Pour in the heavy cream and season the soup with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if you want extra heat).

7. Allow the soup to heat through, but do not let it boil once the cream has been added.

Serve

Ladle the Zuppa Toscana into bowls and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese.

ingredients

1 pound Italian sausage (spicy or mild, as per your preference)

1 onion, chopped

4-5 slices of bacon, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

3 large russet potatoes, peeled and diced

4 cups chicken broth

2 cups water

2 cups kale, chopped

1 cup heavy cream

Salt and black pepper to taste

Red pepper flakes (optional, for extra heat)

Grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)
