Bacon Egg and Cheese

A bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich is a delicious breakfast classic. It typically consists of crispy bacon, a scrambled egg, and a slice of melted cheese, all nestled between a toasted bagel. The combination of savory bacon, creamy egg, and gooey cheese creates a mouthwatering, satisfying meal that's perfect for starting your day.

prep time

5 min

cook time

15 min

serving

1 servings

instructions

Step 1

In a skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until it's crispy. Remove the bacon from the skillet and place it on paper towels to drain excess grease.

Step 2

In a bowl, beat the eggs and season them with a pinch of salt and pepper.

Step 3

In the same skillet with the bacon grease, pour in the beaten eggs. Cook the eggs over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until they're soft and slightly creamy.

Step 4

While the eggs are cooking, toast the sliced everything seasoning bagel halves in a toaster or under a broiler until they're golden brown.

Step 5

Place a slice of cheese on each bagel half to let it melt slightly from the residual heat.

Step 6

Once the eggs are cooked to your liking, place them on the bottom half of the bagel.

Step 7

Top the eggs with the crispy bacon slices.

Step 8

Place the other bagel half with melted cheese on top to create a sandwich.

ingredients

2 Slices of Bacon

2 Large Eggs

Salt and Pepper to taste

1 everything seasoning bagel, sliced in half

2 slices of your favorite cheese (e.g., cheddar, American, Swiss)
