Crispy Chicken Sandwich

This dish is a delightful duo: a crispy chicken sandwich and creamy mashed potatoes with rich gravy. The sandwich offers a satisfying crunch, while the mashed potatoes provide comforting creaminess, all tied together with a savory gravy. It's the perfect blend of textures and flavors, delivering homestyle comfort in every bite.

prep time

20 min

cook time

30 min

serving

2 servings

instructions

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

1. Preheat your frying oil in a deep skillet to 350°F (175°C).

2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.

3. In one bowl, place the flour. In another, beat the eggs. In a third bowl, combine the breadcrumbs with paprika, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.

4. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, then dip into the beaten eggs, and coat with the breadcrumb mixture.

5. Carefully place the breaded chicken breasts into the hot oil and fry for about 5-6 minutes per side, or until they're golden brown and cooked through. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.

6. Toast the hamburger buns in the oven or on the skillet.

Mashed Potatoes

1. Boil the peeled and cubed potatoes in a large pot of salted water until they're tender, about 15-20 minutes.

2. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Mash them with butter, milk, salt, and black pepper until creamy and smooth.

Gravy

1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to form a roux.

2. Gradually whisk in the chicken or beef broth until the mixture thickens. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Serve

1. Assemble your crispy chicken sandwiches with lettuce, tomato, and your choice of condiments.

2. Serve the sandwiches alongside a generous helping of creamy mashed potatoes and a drizzle of rich gravy.

ingredients

Crispy Chicken Sandwich

2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 eggs, beaten

1 cup breadcrumbs

1 teaspoon paprika

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Vegetable oil for frying

4 hamburger buns

Lettuce, tomato, and condiments of your choice

Mashed Potatoes

4 large russet potatoes, peeled and cubed

4 tablespoons butter

1/2 cup milk

Salt and black pepper to taste

Gravy

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 cup chicken or beef broth

Salt and black pepper to taste
