Brown Sugar Turkey Bacon

Brown sugar turkey bacon is a sweet and savory twist on traditional bacon. Lean turkey bacon is coated in brown sugar, creating a caramelized, crispy, and slightly sticky texture. This unique combination of flavors makes it a delightful addition to breakfast or other dishes, offering a satisfying and indulgent taste.

prep time

5 min

cook time

15 min

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

step 2

Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil to make cleanup easier. You can also place a wire rack on the baking sheet if you have one. If not, you can cook the bacon directly on the foil.

step 3

Lay out the turkey bacon slices on the baking sheet or wire rack. Make sure they're not overlapping.

step 4

Sprinkle the brown sugar evenly over the bacon slices. You can press it down slightly to help it adhere.

step 5

If you want to minimize sticking, you can lightly spray the rack or foil with cooking spray, but it's optional.

step 6

Place the bacon in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until it's crispy and the brown sugar has caramelized.

step 7

Keep a close eye on the bacon as it can burn quickly once it starts to caramelize. The exact cooking time may vary depending on your oven and the thickness of the bacon, so monitor it carefully.

step 8

When the bacon is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. The brown sugar will become crisp as it cools.

step 9

Serve your homemade brown sugar turkey bacon with breakfast or as a quick snack!

ingredients

8 slices of turkey bacon

1/4 cup brown sugar

Cooking spray (optional)
